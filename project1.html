WDOD24

Web Design

Webflow dev.

WDOD24 is a university event that brings together enthusiasts of front-end development, web design, and UI/UX design.

It creates opportunities for students and helps companies find new talent. The goal of the website was to connect students and companies, so I communicated with both through the site. I showcased student projects to companies and showed students where they would get opportunities to work and what new skills they would learn. All of this was done with a modern design and a 3D model, integrated through collaboration with Spline and Webflow
